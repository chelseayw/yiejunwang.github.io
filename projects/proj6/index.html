Category
Home Is.In is a personal and experimental online project based off my family's concepts of home. It is meant to be a reading experience that expresses the dispersed, entangled, yet somehow unified manner of our interactions. Visit the project here, best viewed in full screen mode.

This website follows the convoluted and indeterminate structure of a hyperlinked novel, and reveals different perspectives, location and dialogue upon every click. Colors signify change in speaker, and underlined words are key themes link the pages together. Travel around!
