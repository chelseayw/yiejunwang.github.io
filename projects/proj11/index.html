Salt & Pepper

Logo, Identity
A four week effort into a proposed identity for the Salt and Pepper Shaker Museum located in Gatlinburg, TN. The logo form is based off of the mineral and organic structures of salt and pepper. The visual identity is meant to convey a symbiosis yet duality of the two elements.

I took the opportunity to design a informative landing page as well, introducing the museum as an introduction to the neighboring Great Smoky Mountains and drawing attention to its core values. The subtle use of circular and rectangular elements alludes to the logo.
