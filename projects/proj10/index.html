Reelief

Logo, Identity


Reelief is a proposed campaign and identity system for the conservation of coral reefs.

The logo is a typographic representation of the shifting underwater landscape. The brand identity draws attention to the aesthetic exuberance and natural abundance that reef plantlife contributes to our global ecosystem

Context

Coral reefs are diverse underwater ecosystems often referred to as "rainforests of the sea." They provide a home for 25% of marine species. They protect shores from the impact of storms, provide food benefits to humans and economic benefits from tourism. Coral reefs are dying around the world as a result of global climate change, ocean acidification, overfishing and inland pollution. Only 46% of the world's reefs are in good condition. Reelief promotes awareness of these issues in the application of its brand as well as public and didactic engagements.

Goals

Research, propose and brand a campaign for a cause I'm drawn towards, in a month. Utilize the potential of language (visual and verbal) to consider information distribution to my audience.

Process

In my initial exploration, I explored concepts related to ecosystem, layers, complexity and diminishment.
